About Xenia

Xenia Coin is the token by which the Six38ppn.com platform relies on to run. The Six38ppn.com is the website that hosts the six38 peer-to-peer venture capital platform.

Xenia coin acts as the digital currency of all the offerings of the platform. These offerings include the following:

  • An integrated coin exchange ( Six38CoinTrader).
  • An online cooperative (Reach-Out)
  • The Six38 crypto store.
  • Commodity brands ( Six38CBEI)
  • Xenia capital (Real Estate and Mining)
  • A cryptocurrency lending platform (XENIA LENDING)
  • A peer-to-peer remittance service (XENIA TELLER)
  • A lottery pool (XENIA-DRAW)
  • Gaming and sports betting platform.
  • A mining pool for cryptocurrencies.
  • Petroleum Trade.


Unlike most cryptocurrencies, Xenia is backed by mainstream investments. The benefit of this approach is that investors are aware that even though there will be fluctuations in the value, it will not be drastic because it is based on the fluctuation of real-world assets. These assets are selected by the Six38ppn.com team using a strategic asset allocation strategy to ensure that the risk is reduced while maintaining a reasonable growth potential.

benefits of Xenia Coin?

It is not susceptible to the volatile fluctuations that most cryptocurrencies experience. This is because it is asset-backed, giving it a better stability and ROI than other cryptocurrencies.

The coin uses diversified assets to ensure that the value is diversified. This makes it more resilient against economic shocks as opposed to other cryptocurrencies that are not pegged to real assets.

Xenia con is a highly liquid coin because a liquid investment portfolio backs it. This makes it resilient against economic shocks.

Since Xenia coin relies on strategic asset allocation, the risk is reduced despite operating in the uncharted cryptocurrency market.

Features Of Six38ppn Platform

Xenia ATM

The Six38ppn platform will introduce next-gen ATMs that will act as a full-service terminal. This will be coupled with the Xenia pre-paid card allowing the Six38ppn platform to obtain a market share in the remittance industry. Investors on the Six38ppn platform will be able to make easy fund withdrawals safely using the ATM and pre-paid cards.

Xenia Trade market teller

The Six38ppn platform will feature a mobile application that will allow traders and users to pay for goods, services and other products online. This will improve the payment processing by providing a secure blockchain-based platform that uses smart contracts to complete payments.

Xenia teller/ ride

This is a peer-to-peer mobile application that provides remittance services. This is scalable to allow ordinary users to use the platform for payment processing and businesses can also use it for business2businesss remittance of payment, incorporated in this app is a taxi service application like Taxify and Uber, but it will rely on Xenia coin and run on a blockchain platform. It will solve the numerous security problems that plague the taxi service system. The drivers will receive payments using their public keys while customers will pay using their private keys in a pre-determined smart contract transaction.

Xenia lending

The Six38ppn platform will also provide a lending service that earns investors based on how much and how long they lend their money to the platform. The profits will be based on a cumulative interest. The money will be invested in the different investment vehicles on the Six38ppn platform.

Xenia-Bets and Lottery Pool

There will be a weekly lottery pool on the Six38ppn platform that will allow users to earn. However, participating in the lottery requires the users to buy and use Xenia coins to make bets. The winners will be rewarded through airdrop. This is a segment that is expanding rapidly, and the use of a blockchain system is best.

Invest in the ICO

Since Xenia coin is an ERC-20 token, you will need to have an e-wallet that can support the token. Six38ppn will send the Xenia tokens to the same account that sent the money to purchase the token. There will be a public key presented on the platform once the ICO is launched. All you need to do is register for an account and send an equivalent amount to the public account. In moments, you will receive the Xenia tokens allotted to you through the same e-wallet and for fiat payments after a KYC approval your token will be funded to the designated wallet. This will prevent people from stealing the tokens of others.

Caveat Emptor

Make sure that you only send money to the public account provided on the official site.
